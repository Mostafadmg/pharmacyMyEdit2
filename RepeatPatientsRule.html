Repeat Patients Rule

Injectable weight-loss reorders (simple_repeat) — dose jump limits, recommended next dose, bundle picker & checkout validation

Journey: existing / repeat Mounjaro & Wegovy Mirrors transfer + new-starter checkout rules Patient step 16 — dosage picker

Who counts as a repeat patient? Logged-in patient reordering injectable weight-loss treatment (consultation_type: simple_repeat). Their current dose comes from the prior approved order / PMR record.

Core rule: A repeat patient must not jump more than one ladder step (2.5 mg Mounjaro / 0.5 mg Wegovy) above their current dose in a single order. Example: on 10 mg, they may titrate to 12.5 mg but not 15 mg (that is a 5 mg jump).

Bundle & checkout: The same titration-bundle rules already implemented for new starters and transfer patients apply — ladder contiguity, 1 / 2 / 3-pen bundle ceilings, titration acknowledgement, and Continue gated on validation.

Bundle size rules (same as transfer / new)

🔁 Repeat patient — bundle ceilings

  • Single Pen (1-month): strengths from ladder minimum → recommended next dose only. No strengths above recommended. Higher doses are blocked / greyed out.
  • 2-Pen Bundle: strengths from minimum → one step above recommended (capped by maxSelectableDoseMg).
  • 3-Pen Bundle: strengths from minimum → top of catalogue (capped by maxSelectableDoseMg).
  • Bundle toggle does not reset when pens are removed — patient can clear and re-pick.
  • Continue enabled only when pen count equals bundle size and validation passes.
  • Titration acknowledgement required when any pen is above currentDoseMg.
