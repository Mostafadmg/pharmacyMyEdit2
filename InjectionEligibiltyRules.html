Injectable Weight Loss — Eligibility & Dose Rules

Injectable Weight Loss Consultation — New Starter & Transfer Pathways

Draft v11 June 2026 For Prescriber Review Mounjaro & Wegovy

Deferred eligibility model: The patient completes the full consultation form before any eligibility decision is shown. There are no mid-form blocks.

All rejections are deferred — displayed only at the end, after the full form is submitted.

This document covers the New Starter and Transfer pathways only. The Existing Patient (Repeat) pathway is a separate flow.

4
Transfer Dose Recommendation & Cap
ℹ This step applies to the Transfer pathway only. New Starter patients are always on a starting dose (Mounjaro 2.5 mg / Wegovy 0.25 mg).
4a — Calculate Gap in Weeks

The patient provides the date of their last injection. The system calculates the gap from that date to the date of the consultation.

gapWeeks = floor( (consultationDate − lastInjectionDate) ÷ 7 days )
⚠ Use whole weeks only (floor, not round). 12 months is treated as 52 weeks for boundary purposes.
4b — Dose Ladders

Mounjaro (mg)

2.5→ 5→ 7.5→ 10→ 12.5→ 15

Wegovy (mg)

0.25→ 0.5→ 1→ 1.7→ 2.4→ 7.2

5
Treatment Plan & Titration Bundles
ℹ Patient consultation step 16 — new starters and transfer patients choose 1–3 pens (one month / 4-week supply each). Repeat patients inherit their prior plan and do not re-pick.

🌿 New Starter

  • Must begin at starter dose (Mounjaro 2.5 mg / Wegovy 0.25 mg).
  • May order up to 3 pens in one bundle.
  • Offered titration strengths (Mounjaro): 2.5 → 5 → 7.5 mg only — no 10 mg in the picker.
  • Strengths must follow the ladder in order — cannot skip (e.g. 7.5 mg requires 5 mg, which requires 2.5 mg).
  • First pen in the bundle must be the starter dose.

⇆ Transfer (e.g. recommended 10 mg)

  • 1 / 2 / 3 bundle toggle — choice persists when pens are removed so patient can re-pick (e.g. 2.5+5 instead of 10+12.5).
  • Recommended dose badge from gap rules — not required in bundle if ordering below only.
  • 1 bundle: strengths 2.5 mg → recommended only (no 12.5 mg). Default = recommended.
  • 2 bundle: strengths 2.5 mg → one step above recommended (e.g. up to 12.5 mg). Default = 10 mg + 12.5 mg.
  • 3 bundle: strengths 2.5 mg → 15 mg (full catalogue). Default = recommended + next two steps (e.g. 10+12.5+15 mg).
  • If any pen above recommended: must include recommended dose at least once.
  • Contiguous ladder — no skipped steps (2.5+10 invalid); repeat same dose OK (2×10 mg).
