Very important regulatory points ⚖️

Because you are prescribing remotely, you need:

Registered pharmacy premises if dispensing/supplying medicines
GPhC-compliant online pharmacy processes
SOPs for every condition
Identity checks where appropriate
Clear safeguarding process
Clinical record keeping
Secure GDPR-compliant data storage
Two-way communication
Audit trail showing who approved what and when
Clear exclusion/red-flag criteria
Escalation/referral pathways

If you provide online primary care/prescribing services in England, CQC may regulate the service depending on how it is structured; CQC says it regulates providers of online primary care services in England where regulated activity is delivered online.
